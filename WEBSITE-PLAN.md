# Unified Sanctuaries Website Plan

> A comprehensive website plan for the Unified Sanctuaries regenerative community project.

---

## Overview

This document outlines the pages and high-level content for the Unified Sanctuaries website. The site serves to communicate the vision, attract aligned community members, enable donations/support, and provide pathways for involvement.

## Page-by-Page Content Plan

### 1. HOME

**Purpose**: First impression - communicate the essence and invite exploration

**Content**:
- Hero section with tagline: "A Living Blueprint for Regenerative Living & Community Resilience"
- Brief 2-3 sentence mission statement
- Visual overview of the four platforms (Farm, Retreat, Venue, Living Space)
- Call-to-action buttons: "Learn More" | "Get Involved" | "Support Us"
- Newsletter signup
- Current status/phase indicator

---

### 3. VISION

#### 3.1 The Four Platforms
**Purpose**: Detail each interconnected platform

**Content**:

**Permaculture Farm & Education Center**
- Regenerative agriculture and food sovereignty
- Orchards, vegetable gardens, herb cultivation, mushroom growing
- Farm stand and CSA program
- Educational workshops and agritourism
- Compost operation
- Portion of harvest for local food-insecure community

**Retreat & Healing Arts Center**
- Immersive wellness retreats
- Healing arts sessions (somatic therapy, bodywork, art therapy)
- Bathhouse and spa facilities
- Campground for overnight guests
- Dance space and creative studios
- Scholarship fund for accessibility

**Event Venue**
- Indoor/outdoor event spaces available for rent
- AV and event production services
- Festivals, concerts, markets, weddings
- Affordable rates for local entrepreneurs and artists
- Innovation hub for regenerative practices

**Community Living Space**
- 20+ home neighborhood
- Shared amenities (kitchen, bathrooms, laundry)
- Tiny homes, van life support, visitor accommodations
- Pathways to residency through contribution
- Long-term stewardship model

#### 3.2 Phases of Development
**Purpose**: Show the roadmap

**Content**:
- Phase 0: Land acquisition and first dwelling
- Phase 1: Basic infrastructure and resident stability
- Phase 2: Farm systems, cafe, and foundational businesses
- Phase 3: Retreat center, bathhouse, event venue build-out
- Phase 4: Maturity, bioregional service, network expansion

#### 3.3 Open Source Blueprint
**Purpose**: Communicate replication intention

**Content**:
- All governance frameworks shared openly
- Economic models documented for others
- Lessons learned published regularly
- Support for other communities wanting to replicate
- Network vision for connected eco-villages

---

### 4. GOVERNANCE

#### 4.1 How We Operate
**Purpose**: Explain the governance structure

**Content**:
- Two-realm model: Residential + Commons/Enterprise
- Platform autonomy with shared alignment
- Decision-making tied to active participation
- Trust structure protecting land from speculation
- Community ownership without individual land deeds

#### 4.2 Anchor Circle
**Purpose**: Explain the central coordination body

**Content**:
- Rotating, elected guiding body
- Representatives from each platform
- Double-linking for two-way communication
- Role: uphold values, facilitate collaboration, steward whole-system decisions
- Relationship to trustees and fiduciary oversight

#### 4.3 Community Agreements
**Purpose**: Share the values and non-negotiables

**Content**:
- Core agreements (nonviolent communication, consent, accountability)
- Conflict resolution and restorative justice approach
- What the community values (sustainable living, communal meals, healing arts, etc.)
- Blooming period for new members
- Membership pathways (earned vs. invited)

### 5. GET INVOLVED

**Content**:

**Farm Membership**
- Receive share of harvest when operational
- 15% off farm store items
- Priority for farm events and workshops

**Retreat Center Membership**
- Free monthly wellness gatherings
- 15% off retreat store items
- Early access to retreat bookings

**Venue Membership**
- 15% off venue rentals and production services
- Monthly networking events for event producers
- Priority booking

#### 5.2 Live or Work Onsite
**Purpose**: Attract future residents and enterprise operators

**Content**:
- Expression of interest form for future residency
- Application to build/place a home
- Application to operate a business on community land
- Contribution pathways to earn equity
- Blooming period and vetting process explained

#### 5.3 Volunteer & Contribute
**Purpose**: Invite non-monetary participation

**Content**:
- Skill-based contributions (permaculture, building, healing arts, etc.)
- Unified Token: earn community currency for contributions
- Work-trade and education residencies
- Lead a building workshop
- Support land search and outreach efforts

#### 5.4 Partner With Us
**Purpose**: Attract organizations and aligned partners

**Content**:
- Host your event/retreat on our land
- Educational partnerships
- Bioregional collaboration opportunities
- Research partnerships

## Design Notes

> **See full brand identity documentation:** `brand-identity/BRAND-IDENTITY.md`

### Brand Vibe
```
earthy · organic · ancient future · healing arts · creative
community · biomimicry · mycelial · flow
```

### Color Palette
| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | Forest Floor | `#2D3A2E` |
| Secondary | Living Green | `#5C7C5E` |
| Accent | Rich Earth | `#8B7355` |
| Background | Cream Canvas | `#F5F0E8` |
| Text | Charcoal Root | `#1A1A1A` |

**Platform Accents:**
- Farm: Soil Umber `#704214`
- Retreat: Mineral Spring `#7BA3A8`
- Venue: Ember Glow `#C65D3B`
- Community: Hearth Clay `#B5651D`

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Cormorant Garamond (elegant, ancient future)
- **Body:** Source Sans Pro (clean, accessible)
- **Line height:** 1.6-1.8 for generous breathing room

### Photography Style
- Warm, golden-hour lighting
- Natural, not stock-feeling
- Hands in action, people in genuine connection
- Close-ups of soil, plants, fungi, water
- Embrace imperfection and authenticity

### Design Principles
1. **Organic Flow** — Avoid rigid grids; allow asymmetry
2. **Breathing Room** — Generous white space
3. **Natural Hierarchy** — Information flows like water down the page
4. **Grounded Feel** — Elements feel placed, not trapped
5. **Warm Shadows** — Soft, earth-toned shadows

- `brand-identity/design-system/components.css`
