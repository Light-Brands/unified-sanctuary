Where Land and People Heal Together

Unified Sanctuaries is a regenerative, land-based community committed to solving for each other's basic needs of food, housing, and community within a supportive, earth-friendly environment.

Like stones smoothed by endless water, we are shaped by our relationships. The friction becomes polish. The current becomes direction. Together, we find our place in the riverbed.

Rooted in Values

The principles that guide everything we grow and every decision we make together.

Regeneration

We actively heal rather than merely sustain. Every action should leave the land and community better.

Non-Speculative Stewardship

Land is held in perpetual trust, removed from market speculation forever.

Equity Through Contribution

Members earn ownership through participation, creating true skin in the game.

Participatory Governance

Decisions flow from circles of consent, not hierarchy or majority rule.

Open-Source Sharing

Our model becomes a blueprint others can adapt and replicate.

Indigenous Wisdom Honored

We acknowledge Abenaki stewardship and commit to reciprocity.
