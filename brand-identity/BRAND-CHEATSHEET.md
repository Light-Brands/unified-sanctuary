## Typography

### Headlines
**Cormorant Garamond** (or Playfair Display)
- Light/Regular weight
- Generous spacing

### Body Text
**Source Sans Pro** (or Nunito)
- Regular weight
- Line height: 1.6-1.8

### Quick Font Stack
```css
--font-display: 'Cormorant Garamond', Georgia, serif;
--font-body: 'Source Sans Pro', sans-serif;
```

## Voice Quick Reference

### We Sound Like...
- Warm, grounded, wise
- Invitational (never pushy)
- Poetic but plain
- Honest about complexity

### We DON'T Sound Like...
- Corporate or institutional
- Hype-y or sales-y
- New age jargon
- Cold or technical

### Good Words
```
grow, tend, cultivate, weave, flow, root, emerge,
gather, nourish, heal, regenerate, sanctuary
```

### Words to Avoid
```
leverage, optimize, scale, disrupt, synergy,
revolutionary, exclusive, guaranteed
```
