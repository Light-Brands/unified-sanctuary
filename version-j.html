The Living Watershed

Four vessels along one underground stream. Resources, wisdom, and support flow between them like water finding its path.

Land Heals People.
People Heal Land.

Unified Sanctuaries is a regenerative, land-based community committed to solving for each other's basic needs of food, housing, and community within a supportive, earth-friendly environment.

Like vessels positioned along an underground watershed, each of our platforms holds something essential. The stream connects them all, carrying nutrients, wisdom, and support from one to another.

The vessel holds. The stream nourishes. Together, they create abundance.

Four Vessels, One Stream
